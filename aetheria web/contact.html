All guests at Aetheria Grand Hotel enjoy the following complimentary amenities:

  • Buffet breakfast at The Highlands Restaurant
  • High-speed WiFi throughout the property
  • Access to our infinity pool and fitness center
  • Daily housekeeping service
  • In-room coffee and tea facilities
  • Bottled water replenished daily
  • Access to our tropical gardens and walking paths

Additional amenities such as spa services, premium dining, and special activities are available at extra cost.

Yes, we offer airport transfer services from Soekarno-Hatta International Airport (CGK) in Jakarta. Transfer services can be arranged during the reservation process or by contacting our concierge at least 24 hours before your arrival or departure. Fees vary based on vehicle type and time of service. Please provide your flight details when booking the transfer so we can monitor your flight and adjust for any changes.

Our standard cancellation policy allows free cancellation up to 48 hours before the scheduled check-in date. Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in may incur a charge equivalent to one night's stay. Special promotions, peak season bookings, and group reservations may have different cancellation terms, which will be communicated at the time of booking. For any changes or cancellations, please contact our reservations team as soon as possible.
