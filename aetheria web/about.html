Our History

Established in 2015, Aetheria Grand Hotel began as a vision to create an extraordinary luxury experience in the highlands of Puncak, Bogor. Founded by a team of passionate hospitality experts, the hotel was designed to harmoniously blend with its natural surroundings while offering world-class services.

The name "Aetheria" was chosen to reflect the ethereal and heavenly atmosphere guests experience when staying amidst the clouds in our highland retreat. Since opening our doors, we have welcomed thousands of guests from around the world, continuously evolving our services while maintaining our commitment to excellence.

In 2020, the hotel underwent a major renovation to enhance our facilities and ensure we remain at the forefront of luxury hospitality in Indonesia. Today, Aetheria Grand Hotel stands as a premier destination for travelers seeking the perfect balance of natural beauty and refined luxury.

Our Vision & Mission
