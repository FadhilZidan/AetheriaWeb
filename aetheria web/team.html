The People Behind Aetheria

Our dedicated team of professionals is committed to delivering exceptional service and creating memorable experiences for our guests. With expertise spanning across various aspects of hospitality, our team members embody the spirit of excellence that defines Aetheria Grand Hotel.

Leadership Team

Keysha Shafa Salsabila

General Manager

With over 15 years of experience in luxury hospitality across Asia and Europe, Keysha brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to her role as General Manager. Her commitment to excellence and innovative approach to guest satisfaction has been instrumental in establishing Aetheria Grand Hotel as a premier destination.

M. Fadhil Zidan M

Director of Operations

Zidan oversees all operational aspects of the hotel, ensuring seamless delivery of services across departments. His background in both operational management and guest relations enables him to maintain the highest standards while fostering a positive guest experience. Zidan is known for his meticulous attention to detail and problem-solving skills.

Farissa Della Agustina

Executive Chef

As the Executive Chef, Farissa leads our culinary team with creativity and expertise. With training from renowned culinary institutions and experience in Michelin-starred restaurants, she brings innovative cooking techniques while honoring traditional flavors. Farissa is passionate about showcasing local ingredients through her artfully crafted menus.

Reza Pratama

Director of Sales & Marketing

Reza leads our sales and marketing initiatives with strategic vision and creativity. His extensive background in luxury hospitality marketing and deep understanding of digital trends has positioned Aetheria as a distinguished brand in the competitive luxury market. He is committed to sharing the Aetheria story with the world.

Maya Indira

Spa Director

Maya brings a holistic approach to wellness as our Spa Director. Certified in various healing traditions from around the world, she has developed signature treatments that combine ancient wisdom with modern techniques. Under her guidance, our spa has become a sanctuary of rejuvenation for guests seeking physical and mental restoration.

Adrian Wijaya

Chief Financial Officer

Adrian manages the financial strategy and operations of Aetheria Grand Hotel with precision and foresight. His expertise in luxury hospitality finance has been crucial in balancing premium service delivery with sustainable business practices. Adrian is dedicated to ensuring the hotel's long-term success while maintaining its commitment to excellence.

Department Managers

Front Office & Rooms

Our Front Office team, led by Surya Dharma, ensures a seamless guest experience from arrival to departure. With attention to detail and personalized service, they create a warm welcome that sets the tone for an exceptional stay.

Food & Beverage

Under the direction of Michael Tanuwijaya, our F&B team delivers exceptional dining experiences across all our venues. From fine dining to casual meals, they combine culinary artistry with impeccable service.

Housekeeping

Led by Ratna Dewi, our Housekeeping department maintains the immaculate condition of our rooms and public spaces. Their dedication to cleanliness and attention to detail ensures a pristine environment for all guests.

Human Resources

Sinta Wijaya leads our HR team with a focus on cultivating talent and fostering a positive work environment. Their initiatives in training and staff development ensure our team delivers exceptional service to our guests.

Events & Conferences

Our Events team, under the leadership of Jonathan Halim, orchestrates flawless occasions from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations. Their creativity and precision ensure memorable experiences for all attendees.

Engineering & Maintenance

Led by Eko Prasetyo, our Engineering team ensures the optimal functioning of all hotel facilities and systems. Their preventive maintenance approach guarantees comfort and safety for our guests at all times.
