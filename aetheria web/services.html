Experience Luxury At Its Finest

At Aetheria Grand Hotel, we offer a comprehensive range of premium services designed to create an unforgettable stay for our guests. From luxurious accommodations to exquisite dining experiences and rejuvenating wellness treatments, every aspect of your stay is crafted with meticulous attention to detail.

Luxury Accommodation

Deluxe Rooms

Our spacious Deluxe Rooms offer mountain views, premium bedding, and elegant bathrooms with rainfall showers. Each room features modern amenities including a smart TV, mini-bar, and complimentary high-speed WiFi.

  • 40 sqm space
  • King or twin beds
  • Mountain view
  • Smart TV

Executive Suites

Our Executive Suites offer separate living areas, luxurious bedrooms, and panoramic views. Enjoy exclusive amenities including a private balcony, premium toiletries, and access to the Executive Lounge.

  • 70 sqm space
  • Separate living area
  • Private balcony
  • Executive Lounge access

Presidential Suite

Experience unparalleled luxury in our Presidential Suite featuring a grand living room, dining area, kitchenette, and master bedroom with a king-sized bed. Includes 24-hour butler service and exclusive amenities.

  • 120 sqm space
  • Separate dining area
  • 24-hour butler service
  • Helicopter transfer (on request)

Wellness & Spa

Aetheria Spa Sanctuary

Our award-winning spa offers a serene escape where ancient Indonesian healing traditions meet modern wellness techniques. Nestled amidst tropical gardens with mountain views, our spa facilities include:

Massage Therapies

Traditional Balinese, hot stone, aromatherapy, and deep tissue massages performed by certified therapists.

Hydrotherapy

Vitality pool, cold plunge, steam room, and sauna to revitalize your body.

Body Treatments

Scrubs, wraps, and facials using natural ingredients and premium products.

Wellness Activities

Daily yoga, meditation, and fitness classes with certified instructors.
